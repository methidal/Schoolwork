Root Words - Spec and Spic

Sentences you can make out of words that have the root word SPEC or SPIC-

  • Spectator- I was worried I would let down my soccer team in
    front of all the hundreds of spectators.
  • Spectacle- The acrobatic feats make a good spectacle.
  • Speckled- The chicken was speckled with
    black and white; it was a weird chicken
  • Spiciest- That curry was the spiciest dish in the whole world!
  • Spicebush- A spicebush is an aromatic dedicious shrub of
    Eastern North America, having clusters of small yellow flowers that bloom early in Spring
